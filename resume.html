Resume

Kayla Rodgers has dedicated the last four years of her life to honing the craft of website design and development and hopes to continue that path for the foreseeable future. She primarily focuses within the realm of personal branding and gaming, working collaboratively with clients to achieve their responsive website goals through innovative design. Her current arsenal includes HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, WordPress, and the Adobe Creative Suite, but she is always seeking new knowledge and skills.

A Delaware-native, Kayla has worked full-time as a Starbucks Shift Supervisor for 8 years. Seeking a career that would allow her to expand upon her creative side, she has diligently worked on her design, interpersonal, and intercultural skills through the online GIT bachelor’s program at Arizona State University as a Dean’s List scholar. Now located in Pennsylvania, she hopes to apply her new skills to a lasting position in the gaming industry.
