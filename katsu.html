Katsucon 2020 Brochure



Katsucon, an East Coast-based anime convention, needed inventive deisgn ideas for their 2020 convention brochure. Within the brochure handed to attendees, they requested information regarding special guests, booth/ event locations, a general map of the convention hall, and other key information regarding the convention operations. We utilized their theme for the year to create a full 23-page brochure encased in a wrapping cover.

As an added bonus, we were to create supplemental materials for the convention. For that piece, we created a table tent for one of the guests.
