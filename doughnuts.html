Daring Doughnuts



Daring Doughnuts, a doughnut shop based in Middletown, DE, requested assistance with creating a website to help drive their business even further. After consulting with them on what direction they would like the website to go in, we were able to create prototypes as well as a finalized, responsive website showcasing their outgoing personality and love for the craft of doughnuts.

This is an ongoing and evolving project.
