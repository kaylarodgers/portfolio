Daring Doughnuts App Prototype



For this project, we were tasked with creating an app prototype for Daring Doughnuts with consideration to the iPhone 12 Pro resolution and size. The app started as a creative brief, moved into 2 iterations of storyboards, and was finally constructed as a moving gif prototype in Adobe AfterEffects. All assets were constructed in Photoshop and InDesign.
