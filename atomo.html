Atomo Coffee Advertisement



The task at hand was to create a complex composite as a professional advertisement. I had recently come across Atomo Coffee in my searches and felt compelled to create something that would blend their current branding and their core value of being a sustainable coffee alternative that prevents deforestation.

Inspiration | Final Product

In the first collection, the images shown are the inspriation pieces, the company's current branding, and the starting images for the composite. In the second collection, we have showcased the final advertisement in three different resolutions and sizes, as well as the logo that was recreated in Illustrator, and the cans that were crafted in Adobe Dimension.

Tools used: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Dimension

**This advertisement was made for practice purposes only and is in no way associated with the Atomo brand**
