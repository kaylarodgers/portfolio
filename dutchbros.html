Dutch Bros. Redesign



Dutch Bros, a beloved Pacific Northwest coffee chain, was highlighted as having a few usability issues within their website. With the assistance of 3 other individuals, we created and conducted a usability test on the website to highlight concerns, and utilized that data to create prototypes of an updated webpage.

Included in the slide show are the redesigned pages of the website, complete with a view of the navigation redesign. Each page of the usability testing report is also showcased following the website redesign images.
